CAMPO TRES VILLA

Nestled amidst the cool and serene province of Kapatagan in Digos City, Campo Tres Villa offers a truly remarkable experience. Imagine waking up to the grandeur of Mount Apo, the Philippines'
highest peak, right outside your window. This is not just a stay; it's an immersion into the heart of nature.

Nature's Playground: Outdoor Adventures


For those who seek adventure, Kapatagan is a treasure trove waiting to be explored. Campo Tres Villa is your gateway to a plethora of outdoor activities. Discover nearby hiking trails that wind through lush forests, leading you to hidden waterfalls and awe-inspiring viewpoints. Birdwatching, mountain biking, and exploring the vibrant flora and fauna are just a few of the adventures that await. Our team is here to help you plan and organize guided treks and excursions.

Luxury Meets Nature: Cozy Accommodations


Our accommodations strike the perfect balance between luxury and nature. Rustic charm blends seamlessly with modern comforts. The interiors are thoughtfully designed to provide a cozy and inviting atmosphere, allowing you to unwind and rejuvenate. Large windows bring the outdoors in, allowing you to appreciate the beauty of the surrounding landscape from the comfort of your room.
